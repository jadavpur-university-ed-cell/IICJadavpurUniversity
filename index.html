About Us

The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, established the “MIC” (MHRD Innovation Council) to promote the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship in various HEIs (Higher Education Institutes) around the country.Under this strategy, selected HEIs will have their IICs (Institute innovation Council), which will co-ordinate with the Start-up Cell and the E-Cell of the Institute, to create an innovation promotion ecosystem. Working on new ideas and transforming them into prototypes will be its primary focus.


IIC primarily aims for
Vibrant Innovation

Enabling students to come up with path-breaking solutions to indigenous problems, and transform them into functioning prototypes, during their formative years

Supporting Start-Ups

Support start-ups, which will be scouted and pre-incubated, under the guidance of eminent professionals, so that conceived solutions become real.

Pre-Incubation of Ideas

Establish a function ecosystem for scouting and pre-incubation of ideas, giving an extensive oppurtunity, which are indispensable for a young innovator.

Develop better cognitive ability

Develop better cognitive ability for our students, which is in great demand in the rapidly changing global market.
